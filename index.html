Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Curiositi ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Curiositi mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information — name and email address when you sign up
  • Learning session history — topics explored, quiz results, and session duration
  • Voice input — microphone audio is processed in real time to convert speech to text for search; we do not store raw audio recordings
  • Device and usage data — device type, OS version, and in-app interactions used to improve the app

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Personalise your learning experience and topic recommendations
  • Track your progress, streaks, and knowledge mastery
  • Improve our AI models and content quality
  • Send optional learning reminders (only if you enable notifications)

3. Microphone Permission

Curiositi requests access to your device microphone solely to enable voice search — so you can speak a topic instead of typing it. Audio is converted to text on-device or via a secure API and is never stored or shared. You can revoke microphone access at any time in your device settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share anonymised, aggregated data with partners for research purposes only. We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • Supabase — secure database and authentication infrastructure
  • Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In — optional authentication providers
  • Google Gemini API — AI content generation (topic content only, no personal data sent)

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained while your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from the Profile tab inside the app. Upon deletion, all your personal data, learning history, and sessions are permanently removed within 30 days.

6. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS in transit, AES at rest) and Supabase's secure infrastructure to protect your data. Access to production data is strictly limited to authorised team members.

7. Children's Privacy

Curiositi is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
