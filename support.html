Frequently Asked Questions
How do I delete my account?
Go to Profile tab → Settings → Delete Account. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.
How many questions can I ask per day?
Curiositi uses a tiered rolling window system. New users get 10 sparks in their first 12 hours, then 5 sparks per 6-hour window. The counter resets automatically every 6 hours.
Why was my topic blocked?
Curiositi is a general learning app designed to be safe for all ages. Some topics related to self-harm, harmful content, threats or hate speech are not covered. If you were shown support resources please reach out to the helplines provided — they are free and confidential.
Is the content accurate?
All content is AI-generated by Google Gemini and is designed for general educational purposes. While we aim for accuracy AI-generated content may occasionally contain errors. Always verify important information from authoritative sources.
Which languages are supported?
Curiositi supports 9 Indian languages including Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam and Punjabi, plus English. Select your preferred language during onboarding or change it in Profile settings.
How do I sign in?
Curiositi supports Google Sign In and Apple Sign In. We do not support guest or anonymous accounts. Use your existing Google or Apple account to sign in — no new password needed.
Is Curiositi free?
Yes — Curiositi is free to use with a daily spark limit. There are no ads. We may introduce optional premium features in the future.
How do I request my personal data?
Email dmshuklaa@gmail.com with your registered email address and we will respond within 48 hours with your data export.
What is Explain Back?
After completing a spark session you can speak or type your understanding of the topic. Our AI grades your explanation and gives you personalised feedback on accuracy, depth and clarity.
My progress reset — what happened?
Progress is tied to your Google or Apple account. If you signed in with a different account or reinstalled the app without signing in your progress may appear reset. Sign in with your original account to restore your history.
